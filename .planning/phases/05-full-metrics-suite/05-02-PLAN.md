# Plan 05-02: Greeks PnL Attribution

## Objective
Decompose each trade's PnL into Greeks components: delta_pnl, gamma_pnl, theta_pnl, vega_pnl, and unexplained residual. This satisfies Success Criterion 3: attribution sums within 5% of actual PnL.

## Context
- **Depends on**: Plan 05-01 (GreeksEngine produces per-trade delta/gamma/theta/vega)
- **PRD formula**:
  ```
  delta_pnl = delta_entry × spot_move × lot_size
  gamma_pnl = 0.5 × gamma_entry × spot_move² × lot_size
  theta_pnl = theta_entry × days_held
  vega_pnl  = vega_entry × (iv_exit - iv_entry) × lot_size
  unexplained = actual_pnl - (delta + gamma + theta + vega)
  ```
- **Data available**: Entry/exit spot, IV from bar data, DTE from expiry calendar
- **Multi-leg**: Attribution computed per-leg, then summed for position-level attribution
